Courier / Supply: Chote parcels, stationery, ya food delivery. 3. Tagging (Categorization) "Resident/Non-Resident" ko hata kar ye 4 tags use honge : Internal Fleet: Factory ki apni gaadiyan (Management + Company Trucks). Contractual: Fixed vendors jo roz aate-jate hain . External Logistics: Baahar se aane wale naye trucks/vendors. Visitor: Personal guests ya interviewees. 4. Mobile Number: Zarurat aur Logic Kiske liye ? Sirf External Logistics aur Visitors ke liye mandatory hai . Kyun ? Agar truck loading bay par choke ho gaya ya chori hui, toh driver ko contact karne ke liye . Solution: Guard screen par "Purpose" select karte hi ek 10-digit field aayegi (Optional skip button ke saath ). 5. Status Field Ye admin dashboard ke liye game-changer hai : Inside Premises: Gaadi andar hai (Timer chalu rahega ). Left: Log close ho gaya (TAT calculate ho jayega ). Overstay Alert: Agar status "Inside" hai aur TAT 4 ghante se upar gaya , toh status Red ho jayega .

 GATIQ: FINAL LOGISTICS &amp; WAREHOUSE AUDIT STRUCTURE 1. Vehicle No. Entry/Exit identification. Vehicle Capacity Tonnage (e.g., 1T, 10T, 22-wheeler) capacity planning ke liye . Consignment / LR No. Maal ka unique paper number jo invoice se match hota hai . Dock No. Assigned Kis loading bay par gaadi ko jaana hai . Driver Contact MANDATORY. Yard mein gaadi move karwane ya coordination ke liye . Entry / Exit Time Timestamps for the log. Dwell Time / TAT (Exit - Entry) . Logistics efficiency ka sabse bada metric. Purpose Inbound/Outbound flow classification. Tagging Partnership hierarchy. Status Real-time operational state. 2. Purpose of Visit (Buttons for Guard) Warehouse manager ko in 5 options se pura inventory flow samajh aa jayega : Inbound (Unloading): Jab bahar se maal warehouse ke andar aa raha ho. Outbound (Loading): Jab dispatch ke liye maal gaadi mein load ho raha ho. Transshipment : Maal ek truck se dusre truck mein shift karne ke liye aaya hai . Returns (RTO): Rejected ya delivery se wapas aaya hua maal . Hub Supply: Warehouse maintenance, pallets, ya office supplies. 3. Tagging (Categorization) "Resident" ko hata kar ye 4 business tags use honge : Own Fleet: Warehouse ki apni branded delivery gaadiyan . 3PL Partner: Third-Party Logistics (e.g., Delhivery , BlueDart , Ecom Express). Marketplace Seller: Sellers jo apna stock warehouse ( Fulfillment Center ) mein rakhne aaye hain . Local Runner: Choti gaadiyan jo city-level local distribution karti hain . 4. Mobile Number: Logistics mein kyun zaruri hai ? Yard Management: Agar ek truck Dock-4 par khada hai aur use hatana hai , toh guard ko driver ko call karne ki zaroorat padti hai . Safety: Logistics hubs mein chori aur mal-practice ka risk zyada hota hai , isliye driver ka authentic phone number audit mein hona $100\%$ mandatory hai . 5. Status Field (Real-time Flow) At Gate: Entry ho gayi hai , yard mein entry ka wait kar raha hai . Docked: Gaadi allotted loading bay par lag chuki hai . Loading/Unloading: Activity chalu hai (Most critical stage). Dispatched: Kaam khatam, exit scan ka wait kar raha hai .
